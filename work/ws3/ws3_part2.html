The different 1/2/3-point perspectives simply depend upon the angle you inspect it from.
1-point perspective is seen in the center, all depth-lines point toward the center.
2-point perspective is seen to the right, lines propagate toward two different positions (left + right).
3-point perspective is seen in the left. This is the general case. Lines can be said to disappear toward 3 different point
